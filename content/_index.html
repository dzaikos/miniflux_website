Summary ¶

Optimized for readability
The page layout, fonts, and colors are chosen to enhance readability on a screen. The content is the most important thing.
Download original article contents
Do you have feeds that display only a summary? Automatically fetch the original article.
No fancy features
Do you dislike bloated software? Miniflux focuses on simplicity. Less is more!
Fast and efficient
Be productive by using keyboard shortcuts to navigate through the application. Quickly scan your unread items with the lightweight user interface.
Your privacy is guaranteed
Miniflux automatically removes pixel trackers, eliminates tracking parameters from URLs, uses a media proxy to prevent third-party tracking, and does not forward any referrer. There is absolutely no tracking or advertising.
Super simple installation
Miniflux is compiled statically without external dependencies. Drop the binary on your server, and you're done. You can also use the RPM/Debian package or the Docker image.
Free, open source, and self-hosted
Miniflux is a free and open-source project distributed under the permissive Apache 2.0 License.
